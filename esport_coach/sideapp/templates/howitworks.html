How it works

Online coaching in just few clicks.

  • Choose your coach

    Our coaches are all specialized in a particular area. Compare and choose the one that suits you the most thanks to their detailed profile: description, lesson proceedings, rate, leaderboard and references.

  • Choose a session

    Choose the time slot that suits you on the coach's calendar and click on it to book. Pay online using your stripe account through our 100% secure platform. You coach is directly notified and your Skype details are exchanged.

  • Enjoy your lesson

    Congratulations! Join your coach on Skype and enjoy your online lesson!
